ABOUT LAUNCHX

LaunchX is a pan-India level fund-raising event that not only aims to bring EMINENT STARTUPS across the nation but also beings in WORLD-RENOWNED MENTORS, thus throwing a spotlight on some of the most promising startups in the country. Through overall leveraging of the entire entrepreneurial ecosystem LaunchX gives the startups an OPPURTUNITY to Launch their Start-ups and at the same time develop necessary skills. The Start-up Pitch competition would be providing fundrasing oppurtunities and provide a tremendous pool of market oppurtunities and resources available. LaunchX exclusively provides early-stage Start-ups the platform to shine and get under te limelight of the Eco-system.

IDEATION WEEK

4th-11th Sep, 2021

Curtain Raiser
Speaker Ideation(Eminent Guests)
The outcome of the previous LaunchX event of E-Summit
1st checkpoint-
Teams of 3-4 people will be allowed
Abstract submission- Participants will be needed to submit an abstract paper of not more than 300 words containing a brief introduction, description of the problem, the methodology applied and the practical applications. Non-elimination round Idea Validation- After 5/6 days- Alumni from recognised organisations mentorship- one on one meeting

PROTYPING WEEK

11th-18th Sep, 2021

2nd checkpoint-
Business Model/Prototype development- Participants will be needed to submit a business model/prototype of their ideas describing how they are planning to launch their idea into the market and convert it into a reality.
Non-elimination round
Mentorship checkpoint from sector-specific persons//slot arrangement
Workshop// design thinking+pitching+importance of innovation in business

PITCHING WEEK

19th-22nd Sep, 2021

3rd checkpoint-
Mock Pitching- Participants will be needed to pitch about their ideas and their implementation
under the mentorship of JU ECell Seniors.

PITCH BEFORE FINAL

24th-25th Sep, 2021

4th checkpoint-
Final Pitching- The teams will be grouped into 3 pools and each pool will pitch to a set of 2/3 Judges(Industrialist/angel investor jury panel).
The top 2 teams from each pool(total 6 teams) will move on to the final round where they will be pitching in front of all the judges.

CLOSING SESSION

25th Sep, 2021

The participants will be allotted points based upon their performance in each round and the maximum points for each round will be 100. The team with the max no. of points at the end of the final round will be declared the winner.
